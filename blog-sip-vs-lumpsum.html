SIP vs Lumpsum – Kaunsa Better Hai?

Investment start karte waqt sabse common question hota hai – SIP ya Lumpsum? Dono ke apne benefits aur risks hote hain.

SIP Kya Hai?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) me aap har month ek fixed amount invest karte ho. Ye beginners ke liye best mana jata hai.

Lumpsum Kya Hai?

Lumpsum investment me aap ek hi baar me ek bada amount invest kar dete ho.

SIP vs Lumpsum Comparison

Point SIP Lumpsum
Risk Low (Market averaging) High (Market timing important)
Discipline High Low
Best For Beginners Experienced investors

Kaunsa Better Hai?

Agar aap beginner ho ya regular income hai to SIP better hai. Agar market correction aur experience hai to lumpsum bhi acha option ho sakta hai.

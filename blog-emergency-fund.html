Emergency Fund Kya Hota Hai?

Emergency Fund ek aisa paisa hota hai jo unexpected situations jaise job loss, medical emergency ya urgent repairs ke kaam aata hai.

Emergency Fund Kyun Zaroori Hai?

Life unpredictable hoti hai. Agar emergency fund nahi hoga to hume loan ya credit card ka sahara lena padta hai, jo costly hota hai.

Emergency Fund Kitna Hona Chahiye?

Generally, 6–12 months ke expenses ke barabar emergency fund hona chahiye.

Emergency Fund Kahan Rakhein?

  • Saving Account
  • Liquid Mutual Funds
  • Short-term FD

Emergency Fund Kaise Banaye?

Har month apni income ka thoda hissa alag rakhein. SIP jaise discipline ke saath emergency fund banaya ja sakta hai.

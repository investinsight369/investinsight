Stock Market Basics for Beginners

Stock market sunte hi log sochte hain risk, loss ya gambling. Lekin sahi knowledge ke saath stock market ek powerful wealth creation tool hai.

Stock Market Kya Hota Hai?

Stock market ek marketplace hai jahan companies apne shares investors ko bechti hain. Share ka matlab company ka chhota sa hissa.

Share Kharidne Se Kya Fayda?

  • Company grow karti hai to share price badhta hai
  • Dividend income mil sakti hai
  • Long term me inflation beat hota hai

Risk Samajhna Zaroori Hai

Stock market me short term ups & downs hote rehte hain. Isliye patience aur long-term mindset bahut important hai.

Beginners Ke Liye Tips

  • Direct stocks se pehle mutual funds se start karo
  • Emergency fund ready rakho
  • Emotions se nahi, plan se invest karo
